# Executable Replay Scope

This public repository includes curated finite replay artifacts for the
24-meet paper.

## Included

- The bounded Lo Shu spectrum and Sagrada ray certificate pack.
- Quaterne transport and permutation-diagonal D4 -> V4 checks.
- Sagrada terminal retraction, terminal duplicate-direction recovery, and the guarded terminal count-shadow `60=32+12+16`.
- The F_2^4 tesseract layer and affine transport counts.
- The bounded-polytope guardrail artifacts cited in the paper.
- The order-four endpoint-24 atlas artifacts used in the paper.
- The exact-V4 affine subclass, affine-normal-layer counts, O5 torsor
  refinements, and O5-anchored Johnson/forbidden-shadow refinements.
- Pytest smoke/regression tests for the public scripts.

## Not Included As Claims

- A universal theorem that 24 is an invariant of magic squares.
- A full Markov-basis or Hilbert-basis theorem.
- A complete Type-A/Tetra extension-tiler classification.
- A canonical Lo Shu/N-poset boundary selector.

The committed JSON files are canonical replay summaries.  The scripts are
computational reproducibility aids; the mathematical arguments and guardrails
are in the paper.
